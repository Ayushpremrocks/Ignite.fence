About Ignite.Fence

Our Goal

Ignite.Fence was developed for Smart India Hackathon 2025 to provide reliable electric fence monitoring for farms, wildlife reserves, and borders.

How It Works

Using IoT sensors, the system monitors voltage, current, and tampering attempts. All violations are instantly logged and shown on the dashboard.

Features

Admins can manage users and track violations, while Users can monitor meters, view power consumption graphs, and receive alerts.

Impact

Helps farmers, forest guards, and security forces detect problems quickly, prevent intrusions, and ensure safe & reliable fencing.

Technology & How It Works

IoT Sensors

Sensors are installed along the fence to measure voltage, current, and connectivity. They continuously send data for monitoring.

Data Transmission

Sensor data is transmitted via Wi-Fi / LoRa / GSM modules to a central monitoring unit in real-time.

Dashboard

The web-based dashboard (Admin & User) displays power graphs, violation logs, and alerts.

Alerts & Reports

Any abnormal activity triggers instant alerts with sound notifications and generates violation reports.
